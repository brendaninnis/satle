Privacy Policy

Satle App Privacy Practices

The Satle app does not collect or track any personal data. Your stats and settings are stored locally on your device. Satle does not use any location permissions and does not require any login or personal information. Satle is built with simplicity and privacy from the start.

Satle Website Privacy Practices

This website uses Google Analytics to analyze traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics collects anonymized data, such as browser type, device type, and pages visited, to help us improve the website and better promote Satle. No personally identifiable information is collected. For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
